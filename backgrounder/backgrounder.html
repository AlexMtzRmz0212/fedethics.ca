about celio oliveira

Celio Oliveira is a distinguished technology executive dedicated to the ethical and transparent use of data and artificial intelligence within the public sector. Currently serving as Executive Director and Senior Manager of Cloud Analytics & Platform Operations for the Government of Canada, he leads teams harnessing cloud technologies and advanced analytics to drive evidence‑based decision‑making.

With over 20 years of experience spanning digital transformation, AI governance, and data strategy, Celio is a sought‑after voice on building trustworthy AI systems. His work focuses on ensuring that innovation is balanced with strong ethical frameworks, fairness, and accountability. This commitment has earned him recognition as a finalist for the 2024 Canadian Data Award and the 2019 U.S. Automation Award. His thought leadership extends globally as a member of CDO Magazine's Editorial Board and as a frequent speaker at international conferences on data and AI.

Fluent in English, French, and Portuguese, Celio holds a Master’s degree from the University of Toronto and an MBA. He is deeply committed to knowledge sharing, helping organisations and leaders navigate the complexities of the digital age to build a more transparent and effective future.
